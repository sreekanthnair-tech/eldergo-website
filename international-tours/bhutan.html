Why Bhutan?

Known as the Land of the Thunder Dragon, Bhutan is one of the world’s last unspoiled destinations — a place where monasteries cling to cliffsides, prayer flags flutter in mountain winds, and happiness is measured as a national priority.

  • Stunning Himalayan landscapes & ancient architecture
  • Rich Buddhist culture untouched by mass tourism
  • One of the happiest countries on earth

Your 8-Day Journey

DAY 1 / 8 Bagdogra Phuentsholing

Arrival in the Hills

4.5 hrs B·L·D 3★ Phuentsholing

Welcome to the start of your journey. Arrive at Bagdogra Airport or NJP Railway Station. You’ll be greeted by your ElderGo Tour Manager and begin the scenic drive in our comfortable AC Toyota Coaster through lush tea gardens to Phuentsholing — the gateway town at the India-Bhutan border. Check into your hotel and relax after the journey.

Drive: 4.5 hours
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Hotel Zhindey, 3★
DAY 2 / 8 Phuentsholing Thimphu

Journey to the Capital

6 hrs B·L·D 4★ Thimphu

After completing immigration formalities, we begin our ascent to Thimphu — Bhutan’s serene mountain capital. The road winds through stunning valleys and pine forests.

In the evening, visit the towering Buddha Dordenma — a massive golden statue symbolising peace and prosperity, overlooking the entire valley. Free time to explore Thimphu’s charming streets at leisure.

Note: Travellers arriving directly at Paro Airport will be picked up and transferred to Thimphu to join the group.

Drive: 5.5–6 hours
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Hotel Tashi Yid Wong, 4★
DAY 3 / 8 Exploring Thimphu

Culture, Heritage & Wine

B·L·D 4★ Thimphu

A full day exploring the cultural heart of Thimphu.

  • • Simply Bhutan Museum — interactive showcase of Bhutanese life and traditions
  • • Handmade Paper Factory — watch artisans create paper
  • • Takin Preserve — meet Bhutan’s national animal
  • • Memorial Chorten — a revered white stupa
  • • Evening wine tasting session — local blends
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Hotel Tashi Yid Wong, 4★
DAY 4 / 8 Thimphu Punakha

Dochula Pass & Palace of Happiness

4 hrs B·L·D 3★ Punakha

We drive to Punakha via the breathtaking Dochula Pass (3,100m), adorned with 108 memorial stupas. On a clear day, the snow-capped Himalayas stretch across the horizon.

Later, we visit the majestic Punakha Dzong — the “Palace of Great Happiness” — situated at the confluence of the Pho Chhu and Mo Chhu rivers.

The evening includes a walk across the famous Punakha Suspension Bridge with serene sunset views, followed by a riverside picnic. (Optional: River rafting)

Drive: 3.5-4 hours
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Punatshangchhu Cottages
DAY 5 / 8 Punakha Paro

Ancient Temples & Bonfire

4.5 hrs B·L·D 4★ Paro

After breakfast, we drive to the picturesque valley of Paro. Visit Kyichu Lhakhang — one of Bhutan’s oldest and holiest temples, built in the 7th century.

In the evening, unwind at the resort with a bonfire, music, and games specially curated for the group.

Drive: 4-4.5 hours
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Hotel Shomo Chuki, 4★
DAY 6 / 8 Exploring Paro

Tiger’s Nest, Farmhouse & Masks

B·L·D 4★ Paro

The highlight of the journey.

Optional: Visit the midpoint of Tiger’s Nest Monastery — hike for approximately 3 hours or take a pony ride for 1.5 hours to reach the viewpoint. For those who prefer a relaxed morning, we visit the historic Rinpung Dzong instead.

After lunch, visit a traditional Bhutanese farmhouse to experience local cuisine with unlimited butter tea and wine. Try your hand at archery — Bhutan’s national sport — and enjoy a traditional hot stone bath.

The day concludes with a mesmerising mask dance performance and live music.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Hotel Shomo Chuki, 4★
DAY 7 / 8 Paro Phuentsholing

Return Drive & Local Markets

6.5 hrs B·L·D 3★ Prem.

After breakfast, we drive back to Phuentsholing, enjoying the scenic landscapes along the way. The evening is free for shopping, exploring local markets, or relaxing at the hotel.

Note: Travellers departing from Paro will be transferred to Paro Airport for their return flight.

Drive: 6-6.5 hours
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Hotel Zhindey, 3★
DAY 8 / 8 Departure

Tashi Delek!

4 hrs B·L

After breakfast, transfer from Phuentsholing to Bagdogra Airport or NJP Railway Station for your onward journey.

Carry with you unforgettable memories of Bhutan — the Kingdom of Happiness.

Meals: Breakfast & Lunch

Questions About This Tour

Is Bhutan physically demanding?
Most of the journey is comfortable with AC vehicle transfers. Walking is moderate at sightseeing spots. The Tiger’s Nest hike is optional — a pony ride alternative is available, or you can skip it entirely and visit Rinpung Dzong instead.
What’s the weather like?
Bhutan enjoys pleasant weather across seasons — spring brings blooming valleys, summer is lush and green, and autumn offers crystal-clear mountain views. Temperatures range from 15–25°C in most months. Your travel advisor can recommend the best month for your preferences.
Can I fly directly to Paro instead of driving?
Yes. For ₹23,500 extra, you can fly directly to Paro and skip the road journey. Ask us for details.
What if I need to cancel?
Full refund if cancelled 30+ days before departure. We also try to find a replacement traveller for last-minute cancellations to help you get your money back. Full cancellation policy available on request.
Is the tour manager with us the entire time?
Yes — a trained ElderGo tour manager from India accompanies you from Day 1 to Day 8. Plus a certified local Bhutanese guide for cultural context and local expertise.
I have dietary requirements — can you accommodate?
We cater to vegetarian and common dietary preferences on all our tours. Please let us know your requirements when you enquire and we’ll confirm what we can accommodate for your specific tour.
