We don't just add a "senior label" to standard tours. We've designed this Bali experience from the ground up for people 60 and above, combining rich culture, accessible sightseeing, and effortless coastal drives.

Your 7-Day Journey

DAY 1 / 7 Arrival in Bali

Welcome & Sunset Cruise

1 hr L·D 5★ Jimbaran

Touch down at Ngurah Rai International Airport, Denpasar, where our ElderGo representative welcomes you with a warm greeting and assists you through the airport.

Transfer to your hotel in South Bali. Check in, freshen up, and soak in your first Balinese views.

In the evening, experience the Bali Hai Sunset Dinner Cruise from Benoa Harbour. Enjoy a relaxing evening on the Indian Ocean with a buffet dinner, live music, and golden hour views of the Bali coastline. The perfect, effortless welcome to the island.

Drive: 1 hour
Meals: Lunch & Dinner
Royal Tulip Springhill
DAY 2 / 7 South Bali

Culture, Cliffs & Sunset

2.5 hrs B·L·D 5★ Jimbaran

Start the day at GWK Cultural Park, home to the iconic 75-metre Garuda Wisnu Kencana statue. A stunning celebration of Balinese Hindu mythology set against dramatic limestone cliffs.

In the afternoon, visit Uluwatu Temple — a sacred sea temple perched 70 metres above the Indian Ocean, offering breathtaking cliff-top views. Watch out for the cheeky resident monkeys!

Witness the spectacular Kecak Fire Dance at sunset, a chorus of 100+ men re-enacting the Ramayana epic through chant, fire, and movement.

Conclude the day with a relaxed beachfront dinner in Jimbaran before heading back to the hotel.

Drive: 2.5 hours
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Royal Tulip Springhill
DAY 3 / 7 Jimbaran Ubud

Bali's Green Heart

4 hrs B·L·D 4★ Ubud

We check out and journey towards Ubud. Our first stop is the Tegalalang Rice Terraces — UNESCO-listed cascading rice paddies carved into the hillside, painting the landscape in a hundred shades of green.

Experience the famous Bali Swing and soar over the jungle canopy. This is completely optional — those who prefer can relax at the scenic café area and enjoy the views.

Enjoy a leisurely walk through a fragrant spice and Coffee Plantation. Sample Bali's famous Kopi Luwak and hear the story behind the world's most unique coffee. Check into your Ubud resort.

Drive: 4 hours
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Swan Paradise Ubud
DAY 4 / 7 Exploring Ubud

Highland Bali & Art Markets

3.5 hrs B·L·D 4★ Ubud

Visit Handara Gate, Bali's most photographed landmark — a towering Balinese split gate framed by lush highland forest.

Proceed to Ulun Danu Beratan Temple, a serene floating temple on a crater lake situated at 1,200 metres elevation.

Spend the afternoon browsing the Ubud Art Market & Ubud Palace for Bali's finest hand-crafted souvenirs.

In the evening, enjoy a graceful Legong Dance performance at Ubud Palace, showcasing ornate classical Balinese movements, vivid costumes, and centuries of tradition.

Drive: 3.5 hours
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Swan Paradise Ubud
DAY 5 / 7 Ubud Tanah Lot

Sacred & Scenic

4 hrs B·L·D 5★ Jimbaran

A leisurely morning at the hotel — relax by the pool, or simply enjoy the views over your morning chai.

Visit Tirta Empul Temple, one of Bali's most sacred sites built in 962 AD around a holy spring believed to have healing powers.

Enjoy a stop at The Coffee Company, Ubud for coffee and croissants. A sweet farewell to the highlands.

Head towards the coast to witness Tanah Lot Temple at Sunset. Bali's most iconic image — an ancient sea temple rising from a volcanic rock formation, surrounded by crashing waves. Return to your Jimbaran hotel for dinner.

Drive: 4 hours
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Royal Tulip Springhill
DAY 6 / 7 South Bali

Relax and Recharge

1.5 hrs B·L·D 5★ Jimbaran

Enjoy a peaceful Glass Bottom Boat Ride to Turtle Island at Tanjung Benoa, Bali's water sports capital. You can also choose to do other water sports on your own (payable directly).

Unwind with a Traditional Balinese Spa (30-minute massage) included to melt away the travel fatigue.

Shop for memories at Krisna Oleh Oleh, Bali's largest and most popular souvenir store. Stock up on Kopi Luwak, batik clothing, and Balinese chocolates.

End the trip with a special Farewell Gala Dinner with your ElderGo family. Celebrate the journey, the memories, and the friendships made along the way.

Drive: 1.5 hours
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Royal Tulip Springhill

30 mins Breakfast

After breakfast, transfer from your hotel to Ngurah Rai International Airport for your flight back to India.

Fly home with a heart full of memories, a bag full of souvenirs, and stories worth telling for years. Suksma, Bali!

Drive: 30 mins

Questions About This Tour

Is the Bali tour physically demanding?
Our sightseeing selection filters out anything that is not genuinely senior-friendly. We use comfortable AC vehicles and there are no extreme altitude concerns or long strenuous treks. Some spots like Bali Swing are entirely optional!
What’s the weather like in Bali in July?
Bali has warm, dry weather in July. It is one of the best times to visit, avoiding the heavy rain months. Expect pleasant sunny days perfect for exploring.
Are flights included in the price?
No, the ₹90,000 price covers the land package. Flight tickets are additional and depend on your departure city (approx. ₹55,000 from Bengaluru, ₹74,000 from Delhi). Our team will book the best available flights for you.
What if I need to cancel?
Full refund if cancelled 40+ days before departure. Flight cancellations depend on the airline's policy. We also try to find a replacement traveller for last-minute cancellations to help you get your money back!
Is the tour manager with us the entire time?
Yes — a first-aid trained ElderGo tour manager from India accompanies you throughout. Plus a certified local Balinese guide for cultural context and local expertise.
Do I need a visa for Bali?
Yes, a visa is required. However, your Visa and the mandatory Bali Tourism Tax are completely included in the tour package, so you don't need to worry about the process.
