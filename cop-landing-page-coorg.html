DAY 1 / 3 Bengaluru Coorg

Journey into Nature's Lap

5 hrs L·D

Early morning pickup from your Bengaluru zone. Scenic drive to Coorg with a stop at Namdroling Monastery — one of South India’s largest Tibetan settlements, home to stunning golden Buddha statues.

By afternoon, they will check into Yellow Bamboo Resort — their home for the next two nights. They settle in, breathe deeply, and prepare for a magical evening.

Evening: Guided nature walk through coffee & spice trails, followed by a campfire under the stars with a traditional Kodava dinner.

Drive: ~5 hrs
Meals: L & D
Yellow Bamboo
DAY 2 / 3 Coorg Highlights

Waterfalls, Viewpoints & Coffee

Sightseeing B·L·D

A full day exploring Coorg’s highlights at a comfortable pace.

Morning visit to Abbey Falls — a stunning cascade surrounded by coffee plantations and spice estates. Then to Raja’s Seat — the famous viewpoint where Kodagu kings once watched sunsets over misty valleys.

After lunch, explore the historic Madikeri Fort and Omkareshwara Temple. Late afternoon: guided coffee plantation walk — learning how Coorg’s famous coffee goes from bean to cup.

Evening at leisure at the resort. Spa treatments available at own cost.

Meals: B, L & D
Yellow Bamboo
DAY 3 / 3 Coorg Bengaluru

Elephant Trails & Coffee Tales

5 hrs B·L

After breakfast, check out and visit Mathigodu Elephant Camp — nestled deep within Coorg’s forests.

Stop at Tata’s Café — a charming spot known for rich, locally grown Coorg coffee.

Enjoy a traditional Coorgi lunch bursting with regional flavours. After lunch, relaxed drive back to Bengaluru. Drop-off at their pickup zone by evening.

Drive: ~5 hours
Meals: B & L

Will they be safe without me?
Your parents will be with a trained ElderGo tour manager from pickup to drop-off — the entire 3 days. We share live WhatsApp updates and photos with YOU throughout the trip. They’re never alone, and you’re never out of the loop.
My parents say they don’t want to go. Should I still book?
We hear this a lot. Most parents initially resist — they feel it’s an unnecessary expense or they worry about being a burden. But our travellers consistently book a second trip within 6 months. The first step is always the hardest. You’re not just booking a trip — you’re giving them permission to enjoy themselves.

Yes! Check the “Make it a surprise” box during booking. We’ll send a beautifully worded gift announcement to your parents on a date you choose — perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, or just because.

We cater to vegetarian and common dietary preferences. Let us know their requirements when you book and we’ll confirm what we can accommodate for their specific tour.
