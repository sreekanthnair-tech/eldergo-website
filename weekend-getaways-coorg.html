Your 3-Day Retreat

DAY 1 / 3 Bengaluru Coorg

Journey into Nature’s Lap

~5 hrs L·D Yellow Bamboo

Early morning pickup from your Bengaluru zone. Scenic drive to Coorg with a stop at Namdroling Monastery — one of South India’s largest Tibetan settlements, home to stunning golden Buddha statues.

By afternoon, check into Yellow Bamboo Resort — your home for the next two nights. Settle in, breathe deeply, and prepare for a magical evening.

Evening: Guided nature walk through coffee & spice trails, followed by a campfire under the stars with Kodava dinner.

Drive: ~5 hours
Meals: Lunch & Dinner
Yellow Bamboo Resort
DAY 2 / 3 Exploring Coorg

Waterfalls, Viewpoints & Coffee

B·L·D Yellow Bamboo

A full day exploring Coorg’s highlights.

Morning visit to Abbey Falls — a stunning cascade surrounded by coffee plantations and spice estates. Then to Raja’s Seat — the famous viewpoint where Kodagu kings once watched sunsets over misty valleys.

After lunch, explore the historic Madikeri Fort and Omkareshwara Temple. Late afternoon: guided coffee plantation walk — learn how Coorg’s famous coffee goes from bean to cup.

Evening at leisure at the resort. Spa treatments available at own cost.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
DAY 3 / 3 Coorg Bengaluru

Elephant Trails & Coffee Tales ☕

~5 hrs B·L

After breakfast, check out and visit Mathigodu Elephant Camp — nestled deep within Coorg’s forests.

Stop at Tata’s Café — a charming spot known for rich, locally grown Coorg coffee.

Enjoy a traditional Coorgi lunch bursting with regional flavours. After lunch, relaxed drive back to Bengaluru. Drop-off at your pickup zone by evening.

Drive: ~5 hours
Meals: Breakfast & Lunch

Questions About This Trip

Where exactly is the pickup in Bengaluru?
We pick up from 4 zones across Bengaluru: Koramangala (Sony Signal), Whitefield (Phoenix Mall), JP Nagar (Bannerghatta Road Junction), and Rajajinagar (Metro Station). Exact pickup time communicated 2 days before departure. Home pickup to your nearest zone is available at additional cost — ask us for details.
What if I need to cancel?
Full refund if cancelled 15+ days before departure. 50% refund for 7-14 days. No refund within 7 days. We also try to find a replacement traveller for last-minute cancellations.
Is Coorg physically demanding?
Not at all. This is a gentle-paced retreat. Sightseeing involves light walking at viewpoints and temples. The coffee plantation walk is on flat terrain. The resort has well-maintained paths. No strenuous hiking.
I’m travelling alone — is that okay?
Absolutely. Many of our travellers come solo and leave with new friends. You’ll be part of a group of 20 like-minded seniors. Solo travellers are paired with another solo traveller for room sharing, or you can opt for single occupancy at an additional cost.
What’s the group size?
Maximum 20 travellers per departure. Small groups ensure personalised attention from your tour manager and a comfortable experience.
I have dietary requirements — can you accommodate?
We cater to vegetarian and common dietary preferences. Let us know when you book and we’ll confirm what we can accommodate.
What happens after I pay ₹3,000?
You’ll receive a booking confirmation on WhatsApp and email within 2 hours. Your pickup zone, time, and trip details will be shared 2 days before departure. The remaining amount (₹11,999) is due 10 days before the trip via the payment link we’ll share.
Is the resort wheelchair accessible?
The resort grounds have some uneven paths typical of a plantation setting. Most common areas are accessible, but we recommend contacting us if you have specific mobility needs so we can confirm suitability.
