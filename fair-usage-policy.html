Fair Usage Policy

Table of Contents

1. Introduction 2. Scope of Policy 3. Responsible Usage 4. Prohibited Activities 5. Enforcement & Violations 6. Amendments
1

Introduction

ElderGo Private Limited is committed to providing a high-quality and enjoyable travel experience for all senior travelers. Our Fair Usage Policy (FUP) ensures that services are used in a reasonable and non-exploitative manner to maintain fairness, safety, and efficiency for all customers.

2

Scope of Policy limited to:

  • Group travel packages
  • Personalized itinerary planning
  • Customer support services
  • Loyalty programs and referral benefits

This policy applies to all customers, travelers, and users of ElderGo Private Limited's services.

3

Responsible Usage of Services

To ensure that all customers enjoy a smooth travel experience, the following guidelines must be followed:

3.1. Group Travel Conduct

  • Travelers must adhere to the group size limitations (maximum of 20-24 members per trip).
  • Respectful behavior towards fellow travelers, tour managers, and service staff is mandatory.
  • Any disruptive or offensive conduct will lead to warnings and, if necessary, removal from the trip without a refund.

3.2. Custom Itinerary Services

  • Custom itinerary services are provided with a planning fee (5% of the total trip cost).
  • Customers are expected to provide accurate details to ensure the best possible travel experience.
  • Revisions beyond the agreed limit may be subject to additional charges.

3.3. Booking and Payment Regulations

  • All bookings are subject to availability and require timely payments as per the agreed schedule.
  • Cancellations and modifications are governed by our cancellation policy.

3.4. Referral and Reward Programs

  • Referral bonuses are intended for personal use and cannot be exploited through bulk referrals or fake accounts.
  • Misuse of referral benefits may result in disqualification from the program.

3.5. Use of Discounts and Promotional Offers

  • Promotional offers, discounts, and loyalty credits cannot be misused or transferred.
  • Customers found misusing offers through fraudulent means will be restricted from future promotions.
4

Prohibited Activities

To maintain fairness for all customers, the following activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Sharing or reselling personal discounts, itineraries, or bookings.
  • Abusive behavior towards staff, fellow travelers, or service providers.
  • Providing false information for bookings or claims.
  • Unauthorized commercial use of ElderGo's services.
5

Enforcement and Violations

Customers violating the Fair Usage Policy may face penalties including:

  • Cancellation of bookings without a refund.
  • Restrictions on future participation in ElderGo’s tours.
  • Legal action if fraudulent activities are involved.
6

Amendments

ElderGo Private Limited reserves the right to update or modify this Fair Usage Policy at any time. Customers are encouraged to review the policy periodically.
